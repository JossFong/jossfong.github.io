Weather and Suicide in NYC

I would like to examine data on suicides in New York City over the past several years to look for correlations with weather/climate data such as seasonality, temperature, precipitation, cloud cover or humidity. Suicide data comes from NYC or the CDC. They're behind a few years, but that shouldn't matter for my purposes. NOAA has historical daily weather data.

NYC Energy Consumption

I'm interested in which residential areas in the city use the least and most amount of energy per capita. Data on 2010 residential energy consumption by zipcode is available through NYC Open Data and I can get population data for the zip codes from the Census American Fact Finder.

Women with Science Degrees

Though a large portion of undergraduate women complete a bachelors degree in science, a much smaller proportion of those who finish graduate school are women. I'd like to illustrate that gap with data from the National Science Foundation.
